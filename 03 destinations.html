Islamabad City,Pakistan

Historical Landmarks

Faisal Mosque: No visit to Islamabad is complete without exploring the Faisal Mosque, the largest mosque in Pakistan and an iconic symbol of Islamic architecture. The mosque's striking modern design and peaceful ambiance make it a must-see .

Exploring the Natural Beauty

Shakarparian Park: Located near the Pakistan Monument, Shakarparian Park is a great spot for a family picnic. With well-maintained lawns, sculptures, and a play area for children, it's a serene escape .
